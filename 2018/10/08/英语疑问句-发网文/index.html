英语疑问句

Posted on 2018-10-08

问句类型

  • 特殊疑问句：疑问词开头，一般用降调
  • 一般问句：系动词和情态动词开头，一般用升调
  • 选择疑问句：前面选择用升调，后面选择用降调
  • 反意疑问句：前面用降调，后面用升调

What

  • what are the habits of highly successful people ?
  • what are the five most important things for a man to learn in life ?
  • what are the behaviors of a confident person ?
  • what are the top 3 people on Quora you’d like to meet ?
  • what are some essential life skills every one should know ?

  • what are some good examples of situations where smart people should play dumb ?

  • what is the hardest truth you had to accept that made you stronger ?

  • what is the hardest thing about being a parent ?
  • what is the one thing you regret doing or not doing the most in your life ?
  • what is wrong with our society today ?

  • what is the one skill that will completely change your life ?

  • what 10 things must I experience in my life ?

  • what can I do to better myself in one month ?

  • what would it be if you could teach the world one thing based on your life experiences ?

  • what should I do ?

  • what should everybody know ?

When

  • when are the toughest times to be single ?

How

  • How do some people aquire such great social skills ?
  • how do I learn to gracefully accept rejection ?
  • how do I stay motivated ?
  • how do you improve your looks ?
  • how do I get my girlfriend to chill out ?
  • how do I become a more interesting person ?
  • how do you learn to love yourself ?
  • how do I tell if somebody is intelligent ?
  • how can I live a more interesting life ?
  • how can I overome my lack of confidence ?
  • how can I find out who’s stealing my money ?
  • how can I stop being average ?
  • how can I change that ?

Do

  • do you care what other people think of you ?
  • do girls really get moody on their periods ?

Should

  • should I unfriend my ex girlfriend ?
  • should I tell my boyfriend about my ex ?
  • should I keep pursuing ?

Have

  • have you ever been mistaken for the opposite sex ?
  • have you ever confronted a childhood bully ?

Can

  • Can you live a happy live if you are alone ?

Is

  • is it ok for me to date someone that I met online ?
  • is there a more useful way for depressed people to feel better ?

Why

  • why can’t I force myself to do the things ?
